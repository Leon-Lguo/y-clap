Overview

What Is Y-CLAP?

The Youth Climate Leadership Action Program (Y-CLAP) is designed to support young people in addressing climate change through structured learning, real-world project implementation, and global collaboration.

By combining knowledge, practice, and communication, Y-CLAP aims to bridge the gap between climate awareness and actionable solutions. We believe that young people are not just the leaders of tomorrow — they are the leaders of today.

Our Mission

To provide a learning and action platform for young people engaged in climate change, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and tools needed to address global climate challenges and contribute to environmental governance.

Our Vision

To cultivate a new generation of globally minded climate leaders who are capable of driving sustainable solutions and shaping a more resilient future for all.

Our Foundation

Core Values

Shared Value Creation

Promoting solutions that generate both environmental and social benefits, ensuring that progress serves people and planet alike.

Collaborative Innovation

Encouraging cross-sector and cross-cultural cooperation to unlock creative solutions that no single discipline or community could achieve alone.

Responsibility & Leadership

Empowering young people to take initiative and lead with purpose — recognizing that leadership is a responsibility, not just a privilege.
