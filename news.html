Program Launch

First Session Recap | Program Launch

January 2025

The first session of Y-CLAP marked the official launch of the program, bringing together a diverse group of young participants committed to climate action. During the session, participants were introduced to the program's mission, structure, and expectations. The discussion highlighted the urgency of global climate challenges and the critical role of youth leadership in addressing them.

Key Highlights:

  • › Introduction to global climate governance and current trends
  • › Overview of program structure and project pathways
  • › Interactive discussions on youth engagement and leadership
  • › Initial team-building and idea exchange

The session set a strong foundation for a collaborative and action-oriented journey, encouraging participants to move beyond awareness and take meaningful action.
